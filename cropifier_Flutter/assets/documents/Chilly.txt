Name - Chilly
Water Requirements - Average summer weather conditions - they will need watering two to three times a week.
Sunlight Requirements - Full sun.
Description - Chilli belongs to family Solanaceae. It is a small, yearly shrub with a straight, branched shoot. It also has a tap root system with very simple leaves. The flowers of chilli are white in color. In India chilli is also called mirchi, lanka etc.
pH of Soil - 6.5 and 7.5
Type of Soil - Sandy loam.
Climate Requirements - Tropical.
Cultivated At - Andhra Pradesh, Maharashtra, Tamil Nadu, Karnataka.
Cultivation Time - September to October.
Scientific Name - Capsicum annuum.
