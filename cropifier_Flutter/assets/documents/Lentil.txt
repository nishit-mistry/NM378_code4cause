Name - Lentil
Water Requirements - About 1 inch of water.
Sunlight Requirements - Full sun.
Description - In India, it is predominantly grown in the North, particularly in Uttar Pradesh, Madhya Pradesh, Bihar and West Bengal. In West Bengal the crop covers an area of about 0.7 lakh hectares or about 18% of the total pulse area in the State.
pH of Soil - 6.0 and 6.5
Type of Soil - Sandy loam.
Climate Requirements - Cold (temperate).
Cultivated At - Bihar, Uttar Pradesh, Rajasthan.
Cultivation Time - April.
Scientific Name - Lens culinaris.
