Name - Tea
Water Requirements - Two or three times per week during the summer, using rainwater whenever possible. Allow the soil to dry slightly between waterings.
Sunlight Requirements - Full or Dappled sunlight.
Description - Tea plantation in India has been contributing significantly towards the socio economic development of the people of the tea growing regions of the country.
pH of Soil - 4.5 - 5.6
Type of Soil - Light; Friable loam with porous sub-soil which permits a free percolation of water.
Climate Requirements - 21°C to 29°C is ideal.
Cultivated At - Darjeeling, Tamil Nadu, Munnar, Assam, Himachal Pradesh, Karnataka, West Bengal.
Cultivation Time - April – June and October – December.
Scientific Name - Camellia sinensis.
