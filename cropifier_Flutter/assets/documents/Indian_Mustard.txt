Name - Indian Mustard
Water Requirements - Requires about 31-40 cms of water.
Sunlight Requirements - Full sun or Partial shade.
Description - Mustard has been a traditionally important oilseed crop in the India. It is a major Rabi crop.
pH of Soil - 6.0 to 7.5
Type of Soil - Sandy loam.
Climate Requirements - Temperate.
Cultivated At - Rajasthan, Uttar Pradesh, Haryana, Madhya Pradesh, Gujarat.
Cultivation Time - October.
Scientific Name - Brassica juncea.
