Name - Fenugreek
Water Requirements - Water the plant with about 1 inch of water per week.
Sunlight Requirements - Full sun to Partial shade.
Description - Fenugreek is popularly known as “methi” in India and used as condiments and as a flavoring agent for food preparations. The young pods and leaves are used as vegetable consumed in daily cooking.
pH of Soil - 6.1 - 7
Type of Soil - Loamy or sandy loam soils with good drainage.
Climate Requirements - Moderately cool, frost-free, and clear sky.
Cultivated At - Rajasthan, Tamil Nadu, Gujarat, Madhya Pradesh, Punjab & Uttar Pradesh.
Cultivation Time - first fortnight of October, second fortnight of June to July-end.
Scientific Name - Trigonella foenum-graecum.
