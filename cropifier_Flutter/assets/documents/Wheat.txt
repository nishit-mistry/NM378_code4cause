Name - Wheat
Water Requirements - 450-650mm.
Sunlight Requirements - Full sun.
Description - Wheat occupies the prime position among the food crops in the world. In India, it is the second important food crop being next to rice and contributes to the total foodgrain production of the country to the extent of about 25%.
pH of Soil - 6.0 and 7.0
Type of Soil - Loamy soil.
Climate Requirements - Low humidity.
Cultivated At - Punjab, Uttar Pradesh, Haryana, Madhya Pradesh, rajashthan, bihar.
Cultivation Time - October- December.
Scientific Name - Triticum.
