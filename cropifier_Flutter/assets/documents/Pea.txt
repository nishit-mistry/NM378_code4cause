Name - Pea
Water Requirements - 1 inch of water per week.
Sunlight Requirements - Full sun.
Description - Green Peas, also popularly known as “garden peas” is one of the vegetable crops in India and basically this crop is cultivated for its green pods. Green peas belong to Leguminaceae family. Green Peas are used in vegetable cooking's, in soups & frozen canned food as well.
pH of Soil - 6.5 – 7.0
Type of Soil - Fertile, sandy loam that drains well.
Climate Requirements - Temperate.
Cultivated At - Himachal Pradesh, Madhya Pradesh, Rajasthan, Maharashtra, Punjab, Haryana, Karnataka and Bihar.
Cultivation Time - Oct to November, middle of March to May end.
Scientific Name - Pisum sativum.
