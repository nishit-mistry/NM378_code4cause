Name - Cotton
Water Requirements - 800-1000 mm.
Sunlight Requirements - Full sun.
Description - Cotton is an immensely important crop for the sustainable economy of India and livelihood of the Indian cotton farming community. It is cultivated in about 312 lakh hectares across the world and in around 117 lakh hectares in the country.
pH of Soil - 7.0 to 8.0
Type of Soil - Black soil.
Climate Requirements - Tropical.
Cultivated At - Punjab, Haryana, Maharashtra, Tamilnadu, Madhya Pradesh, Gujarat.
Cultivation Time - April to May.
Scientific Name - Gossypium.
