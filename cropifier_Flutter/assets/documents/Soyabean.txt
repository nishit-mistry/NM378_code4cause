Name - Soyabean
Water Requirements - 450 - 700 mm.
Sunlight Requirements - Full sun.
Description - In India, soybean is predominantly grown as a rainfed crop covering the states of Madhya Pradesh.
pH of Soil - 6.7 - 7.2
Type of Soil - Well-drained, Loamy Soil.
Climate Requirements - Warm and moist climate and grown in monsoon season.
Cultivated At - Madhya Pradesh, Uttar Pradesh Maharashtra and Gujarat, Himachal Pradesh, Punjab and Delhi.
Cultivation Time - June to October.
Scientific Name - Glycine max.
