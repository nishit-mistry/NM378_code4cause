Name - Turmeric
Water Requirements - Water it about every two days, and spray it with a mist bottle between waterings.
Sunlight Requirements - Partial sun.
Description - Turmeric - The ancient and sacred spice of India known as ‘Indian saffron’ is an important commercial spice crop grown in India.
pH of Soil - 4.5 - 7.5
Type of Soil - Well-drained sandy or clay loam soils.
Climate Requirements - Warm and humid climate.
Cultivated At - Andhra Pradesh, Tamil Nadu, Orissa, Karnataka, West Bengal, Gujarat, Meghalaya, Maharashtra, Assam.
Cultivation Time - mid-December and mid-March.
Scientific Name - Curcuma longa.
