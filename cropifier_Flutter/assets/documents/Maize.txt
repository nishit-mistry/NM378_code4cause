Name - Maize
Water Requirements - A vigorous growing maize plant requires about 2-3 litres of water per day during peak growing period or an average consumptive use of water varies from 2.5 to 4.3 mm/day.
Sunlight Requirements - Full sun.
Description - Maize, also known as corn in the US, Canada & Australia is the most widely produced crop all over the world. Maize have carbohydrate(70%) , protein(10%) and oil(4%).
pH of Soil - 5.8 and 6.8
Type of Soil - Sandy loam, Red soil.
Climate Requirements - Sunny.
Cultivated At - Uttar Pradesh, Rajasthan, Madhya Pradesh, Bihar, Himachal Pradesh.
Cultivation Time - May to June.
Scientific Name - Zea mays.
