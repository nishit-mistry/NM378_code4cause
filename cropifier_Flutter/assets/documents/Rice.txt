Name - Rice
Water Requirements - The daily consumptive use of rice varies from 6-10 mm and total water is ranges from 1100 to 1250 mm.
Sunlight Requirements - Full sun.
Description - Rice is grown almost throughout the year in hot and humid regions of eastern and southern parts of India where two to three crops in a year are not uncommon. But in the northern and hilly parts of the country, the winters are too cold for rice cultivation and only one crop is grown in those areas.
pH of Soil - 6.0 - 7
Type of Soil - Loamy soil.
Climate Requirements - Hot and humid.
Cultivated At - West Bengal, Bihar, Odisha, Uttar Pradesh, Assam, Madhya Pradesh.
Cultivation Time - June to July.
Scientific Name - Oryza sativa.
