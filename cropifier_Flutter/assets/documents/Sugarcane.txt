Name - Sugarcane
Water Requirements - 1200 to 3500 mm.
Sunlight Requirements - Full sun.
Description - Sugarcane is grown as a Kharif Crop. It needs hot and humid climate with an average temperature of 21°C to 27°C. Sugarcane can grow in any soil which can retain moisture.
pH of Soil - 5 to 8.5
Type of Soil - Loamy soil, Black soil.
Climate Requirements - Tropical.
Cultivated At - Uttar Pradesh, Maharashtra, Karnataka.
Cultivation Time - October, February-March, July.
Scientific Name - Saccharum officinarum.
